About Us

car washing and detailing

BF Auto Detail began in 2019, when Bruno Sousa realized the opportunity to create a company that did not simply wash cars, but that went far beyond that, that took care of each vehicle in a personalized way, creating relationships with its customers who several times became friends. After a few years, BF has become a reference when it comes to mobile automotive aesthetics, having served more than 1k customers within palm beach and broward counties, today we have full competence to take care of your company's vehicles. Using high quality and performance products, trained staff, BF Auto Detail is in constant search for improvement and new techniques to take better care of their vehicles.

What We Do

Premium Washing Services

Ceramic Coating

That long-lasting protection is the main reason car owners choose a ceramic coating over the other options. Essentially a hard shell, a ceramic coating will prevent water stains, road grime, bird droppings, and other substances from reaching and harming the paint. With a quick rinse, they'll simply slide right off.

Paint Correction

Paint correction is a widely used term for polishing imperfections, swirl marks and scratches out of clear coat and automotive paint jobs

Engine Wash

Regular engine cleaning helps identify problems that can harm your vehicle's performance. You can prevent dirt, gunk, oil and grease from harming your engine's appearance. Prevention from rusting. Keeping the outlets interior passageways free of dirt clogging.

Interior Detail

is a multi-step process that involves a full cleaning and treatment of all interior surfaces, including fabrics, leather, plastic trim, door jams, door panels, and more.

Exterior Detail

The first step involves spraying the car with a specialized high-powered spray, followed by thoroughly washing your hands on the car's rims, door frames, windows and all external parts that need cleaning! Painting decontamination with clay bar and finishing with paint protection!

Oversprayer Remover

Removing stains on car paint, caused by paintings close to the vehicle! bringing back the cleanliness, shine and perfection of the vehicle!

Oxy Sanitization

Oxy-sanitization is a process that totally eliminates unpleasant odors, such as cigarette smell, vomit, accidental spillage of liquids and food. The process also reduces contamination of the refrigeration system, improving air quality and preserving the health of users.

Steamer Detail

Steam cleaners are safe to use on some surfaces in your car, including upholstery and carpets. They quickly dislodge dirt, grease and stains by producing dry, superheated steam. Steam cleaning the interior can offer a deep clean, especially concerning seats and upholstery exposed to daily wear and tear.
